The only restrictions when creating anonymous functions with the capture operator &/1 is that at least one {{c1::placeholder must be present}}, i.e. it must contain at least {{c1::&1}}, and that {{c1::block expressions}} are not supported:

# {{c1::No placeholder}}, fails to compile.
&(:foo)

# {{c1::Block expression}}, fails to compile.
&(&1; &2)
