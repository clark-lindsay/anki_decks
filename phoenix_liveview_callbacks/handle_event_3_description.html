handle_event(event, unsigned_params, socket)

(optional)

Invoked to {{c1::handle events sent by the client}}.

It receives the event name, the event payload as a map, and the socket.

It must return {{c2::{:noreply, socket}}}, where {{c2:::noreply}} means no additional information is sent to the client, or {{c2::{:reply, map(), socket}}}, where the given {{c2::map()}} is encoded and {{c2::sent as a reply to the client}}.
